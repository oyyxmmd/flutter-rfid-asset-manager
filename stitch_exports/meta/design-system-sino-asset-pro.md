## Design Markdown

```md
# Design System Specification: High-Density Enterprise RFID Management

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
**Creative North Star: "The Precision Architect"**

For the high-stakes environment of Chinese enterprise RFID asset management, we move beyond the generic "admin dashboard" aesthetic. Our mission is to transform dense data into an editorial experience that feels authoritative yet effortless.

This design system avoids the "boxed-in" feel of traditional ERPs by utilizing **Tonal Architecture**. Instead of rigid grids and heavy borders, we use a sophisticated layering of light and depth to guide the eye. The interface should feel like a high-end digital workstation—surgical in its precision, but premium in its execution. We prioritize information density through tight vertical rhythms and clear Chinese typography, ensuring that a facility manager can scan thousands of assets without cognitive fatigue.

## 2. Colors & Surface Philosophy
The palette is rooted in `primary` (#005DAA), a refined evolution of "China Tech Blue" that provides better contrast and a more "State-Owned Enterprise" (SOE) level of prestige.

### The "No-Line" Rule
Do not use 1px solid borders to define sections. Boundaries are created exclusively through background color shifts.

### Glass & Gradient Soul
Core CTAs should use a gradient from `primary` (#005DAA) to `primary-container` (#0075D5).

## 3. Typography: The Editorial Scale
Inter is used for English and numerals; Chinese uses PingFang SC as available.

## 4. Elevation & Depth
Use tonal layering over traditional drop shadows. Reserve ambient shadows for modals and floating cards.

## 5. Components
Primary buttons use high roundness and the branded blue gradient. Lists and cards avoid dividers and use spacing instead.

## 6. Do’s and Don’ts
Do use tonal hierarchy, active/inactive icon differentiation, and 8px rhythm. Do not use pure black text or dense divider lines.
```
